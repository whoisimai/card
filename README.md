# Business Card Generator - React Application

A sleek and user-friendly React application that allows users to create custom business cards with ease. Users can select from a variety of professionally designed templates, input their personal details, and download their personalized business card as a high-quality PDF.

#Features
- Template Selection: Choose from multiple customizable business card templates to suit different styles and needs.
- Personalized Input: Add your name, title, company name, contact details, and more.
- Real-Time Preview: See your updates reflected instantly as you customize your card.
- PDF Download: Download your finished business card in a high-quality PDF format, ready to print or share.
- Responsive Design: Works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices.
  
#Tech Stack
- Frontend: React.js (with hooks and context for state management)
- Styling: CSS 
- PDF Generation: Used libraries jsPDF and html-to-pdf for exporting the cards
- Hosting: Easily deployed with Vercel
